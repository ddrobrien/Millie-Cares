Why send you dog to day care?

Sending your dog to doggy day care is a great way for your fur baby to get mental stimulation and exercise. Dogs that may struggle with being alone or have destructive behaviour such as , barking or chewing, Mille Cares Play Zone will help teach your dog more appropriate skills.

What are the benefits to the owner

The benefits for the owners, especially if the dog has separation anxiety, is the they are constantly around the Play Zone carers as well as other dogs. Playing with other dogs creates a distraction from missing parents, and will help reduce these symptoms over time.

What does your dog get out of it

Your dog will enjoy all the space, water, sleeps it wants. Not to mention as much playing that your dog wants. At Millie Cares Play Zone, you dog will be able to enjoy a wide variety of different interactions with fur friends as well as human friends to improve their behaviours, but ultimately have fun doing it.

Happy dog, happy life

Happy dogs, make for a happy life, if you know your dog is well looked after, and getting plenty of stimulation through play and learning techniques, then this content pet will make home life much more enjoyable for all.
